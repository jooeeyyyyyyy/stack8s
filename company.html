About Stack8s

Building the foundation for sovereign AI. Empowering organizations to deploy and manage AI workloads with complete autonomy across any cloud infrastructure.

Our Mission

Stack8s is the cloud-native platform for sovereign AI infrastructure. We believe that organizations should have complete control over where and how their AI workloads run—without vendor lock-in or compromise.

Sovereignty First

We enable organizations to deploy AI infrastructure with complete data residency, regulatory compliance, and operational autonomy.

Cloud-Native by Design

Built on Kubernetes best practices, Stack8s provides a modular, scalable, and secure environment for AI workloads across any cloud or infrastructure.

Expertise & Support

Backed by decades of experience in cloud infrastructure and AI/ML. We provide comprehensive support to ensure your success.
